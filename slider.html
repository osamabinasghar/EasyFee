Key Features of EasyFee

  • Customized Fee Structure

    Design school and class-specific fee structures, including both one-time and recurring fee categories.

  • Dashboard

    EasyFee's software includes comprehensive standard reports on fees paid, due, and discounts across classes, sections, and time periods. Users can also quickly build custom reports.

  • Role-Based Access

    Ensure information integrity with role-based access and rights within the fee management system.

  • Split Payment

    Offer flexible fee collection options, allowing payments to be received in multiple bank accounts as per the school's requirements.

  • Timely Remainders

    Send and receive reminders about upcoming fee installments, missed payments, due dates, and more.

  • Integrate Payment Gateway

    Enhance the parent experience by integrating financing options and enabling online payments through the app.

  • Flexible Discounts/Scholorships

    Tailor discounts to meet the specific needs of different sections and individual students.

  • Customizable Charges

    Set up fines that cater to the requirements of various sections and individual students.
