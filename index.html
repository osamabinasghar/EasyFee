About Application

EasyFee is a premier fee management solution designed to simplify fee collection through paperless bookkeeping. Eliminate manual workload with our user-friendly system, which is easy to use whether you have extensive experience or no prior knowledge of accounts. Effortlessly create fee structures, receive payments, and generate fee dues.

Easy & Customizable Receipts

Generate fee receipts in multiple customizable layouts. Receipts are automatically generated for online payments, and for offline payments, they are created as soon as the admin marks the fee as collected.

Fully Integration with

EasyFee is a comprehensive Fee Management System that seamlessly integrates with iSchool, the advanced School Management System. This integration enables schools to efficiently manage and automate all aspects of fee collection and administration, from fee structure setup to payment tracking and reporting. With EasyFee, schools can ensure accurate, real-time financial data, minimize manual errors, and streamline processes such as invoicing, receipts, and outstanding fee alerts. The integration with iSchool also allows for easy synchronization of student data, ensuring a unified platform that enhances the overall administrative efficiency of the institution.

Key Features of EasyFee

  • Customized Fee Structure

    Design school and class-specific fee structures, including both one-time and recurring fee categories.

  • Dashboard

    EasyFee's software includes comprehensive standard reports on fees paid, due, and discounts across classes, sections, and time periods. Users can also quickly build custom reports.

  • Role-Based Access

    Ensure information integrity with role-based access and rights within the fee management system.

  • Split Payment

    Offer flexible fee collection options, allowing payments to be received in multiple bank accounts as per the school's requirements.

  • Timely Remainders

    Send and receive reminders about upcoming fee installments, missed payments, due dates, and more.

  • Integrate Payment Gateway

    Enhance the parent experience by integrating financing options and enabling online payments through the app.

  • SMS / Whatsapp Integration

    Integrate with Whatsapp for sending vouchers and remainders.

  • Flexible Discounts/Scholorships

    Tailor discounts to meet the specific needs of different sections and individual students.

  • Customizable Charges

    Set up fines that cater to the requirements of various sections and individual students.
